Welcome to Trippie! A one-stop app to plan any of your upcoming trips!
Here is a list of commands which you can use to navigate your way
through Trippie!

[add]     |   Adds a place to your trip
          |   Format: add /n PLACE_NAME /d DAY /t TIME
          |   Example: add /n Jurong East Mall /d 2 /t 1100 to 1400
__________|____________________________________________________________
[budget]  |   Adds a budget to keep track of in expenditure list
          |   Format: budget BUDGET
          |   Example: budget 4000
__________|____________________________________________________________
[buy]     |   Adds an item with its final cost into an expenditure list
          |   Format: buy /i ITEM_NAME /c FINAL_COST /d DAY_NUMBER
          |   Example: buy /i R&B Brown Sugar /c 3.00 /d 2
__________|____________________________________________________________
[delete]  |   Deletes the specified place from the list
(places)  |   Format: delete /p PLACE_INDEX
          |   Example: delete /p 3
__________|____________________________________________________________
[delete]  |   Deletes an item from the expenditure list
(expenses)|   Format: delete /e EXPENSE_INDEX
          |   Example: delete /e 3
__________|____________________________________________________________
[exit]    |   Exit the program and auto-save
          |   Format: exit
__________|____________________________________________________________
[help]    |   Shows a short guide that lists all the commands
          |   Format: help
__________|____________________________________________________________
[list]    |   Displays all places in a timetable format
(places)  |   Format: list /p [/d DAY]
          |   list /p /d 3
__________|____________________________________________________________
[list]    |   Displays all the items bought in the expenditure list
(expenses)|   Format: list /e
          |
